Anvozx

Published Typeface / Motion Graphic / Poster / Process Book

Using the program, TypeTool, I created and published an original typeface called “Anvozx”. My inspiration for this typeface comes from science fiction explorations, specifically space and outer galaxies. Anvozx is a name associated with the Triangulum Galaxy (a galaxy approximately 300 light years away from Earth). The typeface includes uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and over 50 extra characters.

I achieved this feeling of science fiction with the use of geometric shapes and the simplicity of lines. Space between the elements allow for the eye to move through each letterform.

I created two posters to demonstrate the inspiration for the typeface and designed a motion graphic using Adobe After Effects. I also created a book that shows the process on how each letter was formed and the complex precise systems that were used in executing the typeface.
