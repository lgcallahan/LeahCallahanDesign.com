More About ME

I am a front-end web and product designer with a BFA in Graphic Design.

I've established a love for code and recently received a Grow with Google Front-End Web Development Scholarship to strengthen my skills in JavaScript.

I've learned to throw myself into any creative opportunity — you can find me in the studio at 2 A.M. on the weekdays and 8 A.M. on the weekends pouring every ounce of energy into my work. I've learned that as a designer there are times I fail, and hours of pushing through those failures and seeking criticism are how I achieve my best work.

When I'm not judging logos and comparing the UI of app updates, I enjoy Cincinnati Skyline Chili and quoting lines from The Office. I am extremely eager to learn new things, meet new people, grow as a designer, and continue jumping into this creative industry!
